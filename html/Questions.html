How many teams are there?

There are 10 teams in formula one as of 2022.


How many drivers are there?

There are 20 drivers on the starting grid in f1.


How many tracks are there?

There are 23 races scheduled for this year, but there are 25 tracks


Who won last year?

Last year Max Verstappen of Red Bull Racing won the drivers championship and Merdedes won the constructors championship.


How many championships have there been?

There have been 72 world championships starting in 1950


Who's won the most championships?

Lewis Hamilton and Michael Shcumacer are tied for first both with 7 championship wins,


What is the difference between constructors and drivers cup?

Drivers championship is for individual drivers, where constructors cup is for the teams.


How long are the races?

Tracks are no longer than 305km long, and unless there are any suspensions the race can be no longer then 2 hours.


Is there a race in NZ?

There is not a race in New Zealand, but the closest one is in Australia.
